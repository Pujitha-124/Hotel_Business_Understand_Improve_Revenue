## Objective
To forecast future bookings and analyze cancellation, no-show, and lead-time patterns using Power BI.

## Forecasting Approach
Power BI built-in forecasting (Analytics pane) was used on historical booking trends to predict future booking volumes.

## Analysis Performed
- Forecast vs actual booking trends
- Cancellation rate trends over time
- No-show behavior analysis
- Booking lead-time distribution
- Impact of lead time on cancellations

## Key Insights
- Higher lead-time bookings show higher cancellation probability
- OTA bookings have higher cancellation rates than direct bookings
- Forecast indicates seasonal booking patterns
- No-show rates peak during high-demand periods

## Business Implications
- Hotels should apply stricter cancellation policies for long lead-time bookings
- Focus on direct bookings to reduce cancellation risk
- Forecast insights can help optimize pricing and room allocation
